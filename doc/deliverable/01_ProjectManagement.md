# Risk Management

**Scales and thresholds**
- **Probability (P)**: 1 (rare) … 5 (almost certain)
- **Impact (I)**: 1 (minor) … 5 (critical)
- **Exposure**: `P × I` (range 1–25)

Risk level thresholds (by exposure):
- **Low**: 1–5
- **Medium**: 6–10
- **High**: 11–16
- **Very High**: >16
